# PasteFlow help center

PasteFlow is a Chrome extension that turns pasted text into character-by-character typing — for Google Docs, Word Online, web forms, and any editor where paste is unreliable. Paste once into the Composer; PasteFlow types it for you.

Use the **search bar in the header** on any page to jump to a topic. For a larger results panel, open the [dedicated search page]({{ '/support/search/' | relative_url }}).

## Email support {#email-support}

**License, payment, and billing:** [pasteflow.support@gmail.com](mailto:pasteflow.support@gmail.com?subject=PasteFlow%20Support) — we aim to respond within **2 business days**.

**Bugs, features, and compatibility:** [pasteflow.support@gmail.com](mailto:pasteflow.support@gmail.com?subject=PasteFlow%20Support%20Request)

Include:

| What to include | Where to find it |
|---|---|
| **PasteFlow version** | Composer header (stable shows `1.0.0`; preview builds show `Preview · …`) |
| **Chrome version** | `chrome://settings/help` |
| **Destination** | Page URL or product name (Google Docs, Word Online, etc.) |
| **Diagnostic Probe JSON** | Composer → Advanced Diagnostics → **Run Diagnostic Probe** (paste the JSON) |
| **Steps to reproduce** | What you clicked, what you expected, what happened |

**License or payment:** include your Stripe order number or the checkout email.
